Land Information And Computer Graphics Facility

Tuesday, January 1, 1980

Research Assistant Land Information and Computer Graphics Facility (LICGF) University of Wisconsin-Madison, Madison, WI, USA (1988-1993)

For five years I worked as a Research Assistant on a variety of research projects led by Prof. Bernard Niemann, Jr., and other faculty members at the LICGF.

  • Role of the Wisconsin Land Information Program (WLIP) in the diffusion of GIS in the Wisconsin Counties. ** WLIP was a program administered by the WI Land Information Association with the intent to encourage land records modernization at the local level.
  • Ground and surface water modeling of Dane County, WI. ** This research was in collaboration with the WI Department of Natural Resources.
  • Research on the diffusion of GIS technology at the local-level in India. ** Inspired by the work being done in Wisconsin, this research was aimed at studying the forces at work encouraging or preventing the modernization of land records in rural India.
