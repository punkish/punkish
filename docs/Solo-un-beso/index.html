Sólo un beso

for Buenos Aires, where just one kiss, a quick hug, and you are home
Wednesday, August 21, 2013

Dark hair over sharp keys,
Strong fingers caress the sound
Like water tumbling over rocks.

The two friends
Meet for music every Tuesday
For thirty years.1

The dents in the horn
Bend light better than the
Sound, yet the red bar is happy.

Red and green, jazz and pop,
Age and innocence,
Beauty and elegance.

Sólo un beso, Un abrazo, Y está en su hogar

  1. This was at a wonderful nightclub called Jazz and Pop whose walls were painted a deep red. ↩
