Future of Scientific Knowledge Discovery in Open Networked Environments

The National Academies, Washington DC
Thursday, March 10, 2011

A symposium and workshop were convened at the National Academies to bring together key stakeholders in the area of scientific knowledge discovery (SKD) for intensive and structured discussions in order to obtain a better understanding of the state-of-the-art and future trends in the study of computational SKD in the open online environment and to develop a range of options for future work in this area.
